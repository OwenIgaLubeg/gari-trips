6-Day Gorilla Habituation Experience & Wildlife Safari


Overview


Discover the best of Uganda on this 6-day adventure, blending wildlife, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable encounters. Track the iconic shoebill stork, enjoy walking safaris at Lake Mburo, and experience the rare Gorilla Habituation in Bwindi. Relax at the serene Mutanda Lake Resort and end your journey with a scenic flight showcasing Uganda’s stunning vistas. This safari promises adventure, tranquility, and memories to last a lifetime.


Day 1

Shoe Bill Tracking & Transfer to Lake Mburo National Park

After breakfast at your lodge, you’ll be picked up by our Gari Trips driver-guide and head towards to Mabamba Swamp on the shores of Lake Victoria, where you’ll embark on a canoe expedition in search of the iconic shoebill stork. Guided by a local expert, this experience offers breathtaking encounters with this prehistoric-looking bird, a dream for birdwatchers and photographers alike.

After this unique experience, journey to Lake Mburo National Park. En route, stop at the Equator for lunch and photo opportunities. Arrive at Rwakobo Rock, an eco-friendly retreat perched on a rocky outcrop with panoramic views of the savannah. Here, you’ll enjoy a serene evening with dinner and an overnight stay.


Day 2

Exploring Lake Mburo National Park.

Start the day with a guided walking safari, a rare opportunity to explore the wilderness on foot. Accompanied by an armed ranger, you’ll encounter Rothschild’s giraffes, buffalo, hippos, and a variety of bird species in their natural habitat.

After lunch at the lodge, relax with a swim in the infinity pool or soak in the tranquil surroundings. As night falls, embark on an exhilarating night game drive, seeking nocturnal wildlife like leopards, bush babies, and hyenas. Return to Rwakobo Rock for dinner and another restful night.


Day 3

Scenic Transfer to Lake Mutanda

After breakfast, set out on a scenic drive through Uganda’s lush countryside and the stunning Kigezi Highlands, often called the “Switzerland of Africa.” Marvel at terraced hills, dormant volcanoes, and crystal-clear lakes.
Arrive at Lake Mutanda, a pristine alpine lake surrounded by dramatic hills. Spend your evening unwinding at Mutanda Lake Resort, enjoying a delicious dinner and the serene ambiance of this hidden paradise.


Day 4

Gorilla Habituation Experience in Bwindi Forest

Venture into Bwindi Impenetrable Forest for a once-in-a-lifetime Gorilla Habituation Experience. Accompanied by expert trackers and researchers, spend up to four hours observing these majestic creatures in their natural habitat, gaining an intimate understanding of their behavior and conservation efforts.
After this unforgettable experience, return to Mutanda Lake Resort for a relaxing evening. Share stories of your adventure over dinner, or simply soak in the peaceful surroundings of Lake Mutanda.


Day 5

Relaxation at Mutanda Lake Resort

Enjoy a day of pure relaxation amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Lake Mutanda. Lounge by the lakeside, take a refreshing swim, or simply bask in the tranquil atmosphere. The pristine waters, terraced hills, and fresh mountain air create the perfect retreat for unwinding.
Savor delicious meals and the resort’s cozy ambiance as you reflect on your incredible safari experiences.


Day 6

Scenic Flight Back Home

Conclude your adventure with a scenic domestic flight to Entebbe International Airport. Marvel at the aerial views of Uganda’s rolling hills, expansive savannahs, and sparkling lakes. Upon landing, connect to your international flight or spend the rest of the day exploring Entebbe.
