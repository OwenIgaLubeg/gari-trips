5-Day Murchison Falls Safari


Overview


Immerse yourself in the raw power and untamed beauty of Uganda on this action-packed 5-day safari through Murchison Falls National Park, Budongo Forest, and Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary.
Begin with a dramatic hike to the Top of Murchison Falls, where the Nile River thunders through a 7-meter gorge—a sight that defines East Africa’s wild spirit.
Spend two full days in Uganda’s largest national park: spot elephants, lions, and giraffes on sunrise game drives, then cruise the Nile to the base of the falls for intimate encounters with hippos, crocodiles, and hundreds of bird species.
Add cultural depth with a visit to the Pakwach community, and thrill to the nocturnal wilderness on an exclusive night game drive.
The journey continues to Budongo Forest, one of Africa’s richest primate habitats, for an exhilarating chimpanzee tracking adventure, before culminating at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary—Uganda’s only place to track wild rhinos on foot.
Perfect for small groups seeking a condensed yet comprehensive wildlife experience, this itinerary blends adrenaline, conservation, and authentic Ugandan wilderness into one unforgettable escape.


Day 1

Transfer to Murchison Falls & Hike to the Top

Morning: Depart Kampala after breakfast and drive northwest towards Murchison Falls National Park.
Lunch: En route at New Court View Hotel Masindi.
Enter the park through Kichumbanyobo Gate and proceed to hike to the Top of Murchison Falls, where the Nile explodes through a narrow gorge.
Proceed to Pakuba Safari Lodge for relaxation and dinner.


Day 2

Morning Game Drive & Afternoon Boat Safari

Early Morning: Enjoy an early breakfast followed by a game drive on the northern bank of the Nile. Expect to see elephants, lions, giraffes, buffaloes, leopards, and several antelope species.
Afternoon: After lunch, embark on a boat cruise to the base of Murchison Falls, a highlight offering sightings of hippos, crocodiles, and aquatic birds.
Evening: Return to the lodge for dinner and relaxation.
Accommodation: Pakuba Safari Lodge


Day 3

Community Visit & Night Game Drive

Morning: After breakfast, visit the nearby Pakwach community to experience local culture, crafts, and daily life.
Afternoon: Return to the lodge for lunch and spend the afternoon swimming or relaxing.
Evening: Head out for a night game drive, a great opportunity to spot nocturnal animals like hyenas, civets, and leopards.
Accommodation: Pakuba Safari Lodge


Day 4

Chimpanzee Tracking & Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary

Morning: Check out after breakfast and drive to Budongo Forest Reserve for an exciting chimpanzee tracking experience.
Afternoon: After tracking, continue to Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for an overnight stay.
Accommodation: Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary


Day 5

Rhino Tracking & Return to Kampala

Morning: After breakfast, go for Rhino tracking on foot—an incredible opportunity to observe these endangered giants up close.
Late Morning: Begin the drive back to Kampala with lunch en route.
Arrival: Late afternoon arrival in Kampala.
